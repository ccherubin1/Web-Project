Portfolio

Below are projects I created in my DIG 2000C class. I gained extensive knowledge in creating digital content, including designing images using Photoshop, producing videos with Premiere Pro, and developing websites utilizing HTML.

Imaging Project:

For this imaging project, I was inspired by cybernetic philosophy that has guided my academic and career goals.

The utilization of technology to further bridge the gaps of connection between other people and our earth.

Technology isn't separate from nature, it's just a part of life itself.

First Responder:

Stop Motion Project Created with 55 photographs.

For this stop motion project, First Responder, I was inspired by my own preschool ambitions and imagination.

At the time I wanted to be a firefighter, and I always imagined that my hot wheels could come to life and head to emergencies on their own.

TThe short follows a first responder hot wheel doing just that.

Hemani Arabesq:

Adobe Premiere Pro Project.

For this video project I sought to create a mock advertisement for a fragrance dupe of my favorite scent, Atomic Rose by Initio Parfums, that my friends and I found at a local Indo-Pakistani market.

We were quite amused that it was produced locally, so I embarked on making a 70s style commercial of it to lean into its absurdity.
